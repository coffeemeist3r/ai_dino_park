# Cycle 32 — Lore Handoff

**Theme:** The attention economy of a fishbowl. Cycle 31 taught the bowl to be *jealous*
(BACKLOG-120) — your closest dino gets a welcome-back, and a near-tied runner-up sulks. That
left a debt hanging in the air: the slight is filed, but nothing ever makes it right. This cycle
is about **repair** — the keeper learning that attention is a thing you can give *back*. Lean into
the loop where a relationship can be hurt and then healed; that round-trip is what makes a dino
feel like a mind that keeps score, not a state machine that resets.

**Added to BACKLOG:**
- BACKLOG-128 [emergent] Forgiving heart — a dino repaired before (125) files "the keeper always makes it right"; next time it's the jealous runner-up its sulk softens (quicker turn). Repaired bonds learn trust. Builds on 125.
- BACKLOG-129 [emergent] Festering slight — a runner-up left un-repaired across multiple homecomings lets the slight harden into a tiny capped bond-cooling toward the *favored dino* (not the keeper); chronic neglect curdles into dino-vs-dino rivalry. Builds on 120 / 125 / 113.
- BACKLOG-130 [social] Comforting nuzzle — when a dino sulks (😒), its closest dino-friend (highest bond) drifts over and throws a 🫂, easing the funk; friendship between dinos blunts jealousy. Builds on 120 / 013.
- BACKLOG-131 [pokemon] Fondest memory — the collection book surfaces each dino's single happiest logged beat (favorite eaten, repaired, homecoming): a "what this dino treasures" line. Builds on 011 / 021.

**Suggested next-up:** BACKLOG-125 — Greeting the runner-up. It's the direct repair half of the
jealousy keystone (120): greet the slighted dino right after a homecoming and the slight flips
(😒 → 😊) with an outsized affinity bump. Closes the hurt/heal loop and unblocks the whole
"repair learns trust" thread (128/129) seeded this cycle.
