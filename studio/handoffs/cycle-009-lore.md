# Cycle 9 — Lore Handoff

**Theme:** Friendship (cycle 6) only had one verb: *greet*. Stardew's heart is *giving* — learning what someone loves and bringing it to them. Cycle 9 adds gifts: hand a dino an item, and how much it warms to you depends on who it is. A curious dino lights up at a shiny shell; a calm one prefers a smooth rock; the prickly loner shrugs. This is where personality (010) starts to *matter* to the player.

**Cycle status:** Lore-smith bumped cycle 8 → 9. Cycle 8 closed BACKLOG-048 (in-character dialogue), APPROVED. Ninth cycle of an all-night operator grind.

**Suggested next-up: BACKLOG-015 [social] Gift system — give an item to an NPC, affinity changes based on personality fit.**

Why: it reuses everything — the friendship store (016) takes the affinity delta, the traits (010) decide the reaction, the save (009) persists it. Pure reaction math, fully unit-testable, no model needed. It turns the 5 personalities from flavor text into a puzzle the player solves.

**Mood note:** a handful of charming items (shiny shell, wildflower, smooth rock, sparring stick, mossy snack), each appealing to a different temperament. Reactions read out loud: "Rex *loved* the shiny shell! ♥". Loved/liked/neutral/disliked. Make figuring out a dino's taste feel like a small gift in itself.
