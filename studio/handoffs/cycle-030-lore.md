# Cycle 30 — Lore Handoff

**Theme:** The keeper's comings and goings become a relationship. Cycle 29 taught the bowl
to *run on while you're gone* (BACKLOG-106); now make your **return** something a dino
notices. This is a warm, small-surface cycle — one dino, one gesture — but it turns an
abstract "time elapsed" panel into a creature that looks up when you walk back in. Lean into
the part of emergence that's tender, not just political: the bowl should miss you.
