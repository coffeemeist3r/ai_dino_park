# Cycle 33 — Lore Handoff

**Theme:** The court learns to console itself. For three cycles the attention economy has run
keeper→dino: you come home (112), one dino glows, a runner-up sulks (120), you make it up (125).
Every feeling has pointed at *you*. This cycle we turn the camera sideways — when a dino is hurt,
let *another dino* notice and come over. The bond graph (013) has been quietly accumulating since
cycle 18; it's time it did something visible. Lean into the Project-Sid promise: society you can
watch happen between the inhabitants, not just transactions with the keeper.
