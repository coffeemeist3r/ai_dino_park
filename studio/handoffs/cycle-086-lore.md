# Cycle 86 — Lore Handoff

**Theme:** The hatch remembers your face. Three cycles built the contested-drop trio — a well-fed dino
*yields* to a hungry friend (375), a hungry prickly one *gobbles* past (387), a bold one *stands its
ground* (390). Cycle 86 stops treating each scrap as a fresh, forgotten event and lets the outcomes
**accrue into who a dino is**: the loser of a stand-off slinks off remembering it (394), the wins and
losses congeal into a per-dino disposition (401) you can read in the book (402) and hear in the voice
(404), and even the victor can grow a grace note (403). One seed steps off the feeding treadmill entirely:
a dino left alone too long invents a tic that's nobody's but its own (405).
