# Cycle 27 — Lore Handoff

**Theme:** Taste day. The hatch is open and the cast scrambles for whatever falls (cycle 25) — but right now a 🍖 is a 🍖 is a 🍖, and every dino eats the same anonymous lump with the same shrug. This cycle gives the feed a *flavor* and the dinos *opinions*. Each dino quietly prefers one food, derived from its temperament exactly the way gift-fit already is — so when the keeper drops the right thing and the right mouth gets to it, that dino lights up (😋, a bigger bond) and *remembers it loved it*. The keeper learns the bowl's palate by watching: drop a fish, see who comes running; drop berries, watch a different face glow. It's the same fishbowl thesis — the keeper picks the input, the bowl reveals a preference it had all along.

**Added to BACKLOG:**
- BACKLOG-066 [emergent] Taste talk — a dino that just ate its favorite can let it slip in dialogue/gossip ("oh, I *love* fish"), so the keeper can learn a dino's palate by chatting, not only by watching the 😋. Builds on 061.
- BACKLOG-067 [social] Keeper-loaded hatch — let the keeper choose *which* food to drop (cycle the loaded feed, shown in the HUD) instead of a random handful, turning feeding into deliberate pampering. The mirror of the existing `[`/`]` gift selector, for the hatch.
- BACKLOG-068 [emergent] Acquired taste — a dino fed the same non-favorite food many times slowly warms to it (a tiny, capped preference drift stored in memory), so palates aren't fixed forever. Project-Sid-flavored.
- BACKLOG-069 [pokemon] Menu in the book — the collection book reveals each dino's favorite food, but only *after* you've actually fed it that food once — a quiet collection sub-goal (fill in the menu).
- BACKLOG-070 [emergent] Picky vs. gobble — prickly (low-agreeableness) dinos turn up their snouts at non-favorite food and leave it for others; warm dinos eat anything. Personality shapes not just *who rushes* but *who'll settle*.

**Suggested next-up:** **BACKLOG-061 — Food favorites.** It's the queued top of the feeding cluster and the spine the rest of this handoff hangs off (taste talk, the menu, acquired taste, picky/gobble all assume a per-dino favorite exists). Ship favorites this cycle; 066–070 become emergent layers on top.
