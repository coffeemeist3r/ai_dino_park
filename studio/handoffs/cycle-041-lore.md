# Cycle 41 — Lore Handoff

**Theme:** The year keeps turning. Cycle 40 hung a calendar on the bowl — a turning-year clock,
a per-season wash, a once-only "the season turned" beat. A calendar that only *recolours* the
sky is scenery; this cycle the season should start changing what the cast *does*. The freshest,
most emergent of the four season follow-ups is the one that reaches into the daily feeding loop:
the same dino should crave a different food in winter than in summer — and which dinos sway with
the year (and which stay stubbornly loyal to their one true food) becomes a new read on character.
