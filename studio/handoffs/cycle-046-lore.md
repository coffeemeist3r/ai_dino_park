# Cycle 46 — Lore Handoff

**Theme:** Sound becomes signal. Cycles 44–45 gave the bowl voices and a morning to use them — decoration so far: lovely, but nothing *listens*. This cycle the voice starts doing work in the social graph. A call should travel, land on an ear that cares, and move a body. The first listener is the one the lore already promised: the closest friend who turns toward a cry from across the bowl. After two audio cycles this is deliberately the third — but it's not more soundscape, it's the audio layer reaching back into the bond graph and the cold arc at once. Sound stops being something the bowl does and becomes something the bowl *uses*.
