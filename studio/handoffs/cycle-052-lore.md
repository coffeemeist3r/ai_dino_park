# Cycle 52 — Lore Handoff

**Theme:** The bowl keeps its talk honest. For three cycles the gossip channel only ever *grew* — the cold spread, the visit followed, the warmth spread too. Nothing it said ever got taken back. A quiet, satisfying cycle of symmetry: the channel that learned to spread learns to stop.
